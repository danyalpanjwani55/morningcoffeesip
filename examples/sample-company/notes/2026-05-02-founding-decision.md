# Founding decision — Aurora Tea Co.

Decided today: we are building a direct-to-consumer loose-leaf tea brand with a
subscription product at the core. The first product to ship is the "Morning
Sampler" box. Launch is locked for 2026-09-15.

Owner: avery (founder).

Notes from the kickoff:
- The wedge is freshness — tea sealed and shipped within a week of blending.
- We will not do retail in year one; the whole roadmap is the subscription
  product and the website funnel.
- A recurring worry already: customer support load when subscriptions renew. We
  have no support process yet.

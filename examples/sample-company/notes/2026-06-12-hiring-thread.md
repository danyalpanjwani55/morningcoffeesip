# Hiring — open a customer support lead role first

Owner: avery.

We talked through who to hire first. The team today is just avery, blake, and
casey.

Candidates discussed:
- A customer support lead. Support questions (pausing, skipping, refunds on a
  subscription) are recurring and nobody owns them — this keeps surfacing as the
  most painful gap. Strong lean toward hiring for support first.
- A part-time ops/logistics helper to take vendor and fulfillment work off Blake.

Decision: open the customer support role first; revisit ops help after launch.
